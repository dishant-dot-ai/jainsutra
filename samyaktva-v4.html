The First Jewel

Samyaktva (सम्यक्त्व)

"The Single Doorway"

Right faith—recognizing the soul's nature as separate from karma and body. Without it, all practice is ritual. With it, everything becomes a path.

Core Realizations

psychology

Discernment

The ability to distinguish between the sentient (Jiva) and the insentient (Ajiva). It is the sharp edge of awareness.

01

Recognition of the Pure Self as distinct from all karmic association.

flare

The Internal Shift

Samyaktva is not a belief system; it is a fundamental shift in perception. It is like waking from a dream where you thought you were the character, only to realize you are the dreamer.

"The soul is the master of its destiny; right faith is the rudder."

auto_awesome

Alchemy of Action

Without Samyaktva, even the most rigorous penance remains a physical ritual. With it, even a simple breath becomes a step toward liberation.

The Three Pillars

  • 01

    Deva (The Divine)

    Faith in the Tirthankaras who have conquered the cycle of birth and death.

  • 02

    Guru (The Teacher)

    Trust in those who walk the path of non-attachment and guide others with compassion.

  • 03

    Dharma (The Path)

    Understanding the cosmic law of cause and effect, rooted in non-violence.

Meditations

Still the Mind.
Know the Soul.

These meditations are not about relaxation. They are about recognition — the soul recognizing its own infinite nature beyond the layers of karma it has accumulated. Sit. Listen. Look inward.

expand_more

Most meditations on this page are in English or are language-universal. Three meditations by Arun Vijay MS are in Hindi. English meditations will be added over time. Each video is labeled with its language.

Meditation 01 ENGLISH

Jain Meditation Guide — Destroying the 4 Ghati Karmas

By Enlightenment

A practical guide to meditating in a way that works toward destroying the 4 destructive karmas — the karmas that directly obscure the soul's infinite knowledge, perception, bliss, and power.

Meditation 02 UNIVERSAL 🌐

ॐ ह्रीं श्रीं अर्हम नमः — Aum Hrim Shrim Arham Namah

By Suresh Wadekar, Mahavir Jain

A powerful mantra meditation on the infinite energy of the Siddhas — the liberated souls who have transcended all karma and exist in pure infinite consciousness. No language needed. Just presence.

Meditation 03 UNIVERSAL 🌐

Navkar Mantra Meditation

By Nehal Shah

Meditation through Navkar Mantra recitation — using the mantra as a gateway to stillness and soul awareness. No language barrier. Just the mantra, just presence.

Meditation 04 UNIVERSAL 🌐

Logassa — Venerating the 24 Tirthankaras

By Gautam Swami

Written by Gautam Swami — the first and most prominent disciple of Mahavira Swami — this ancient stotra venerates all 24 Tirthankaras and invites the soul to absorb their liberated qualities. One of the oldest and most sacred compositions in Jain tradition.

Meditation 05 ENGLISH

Soul Meditation — After Invoking the Panch Parmeshthi

By The Meditating Jain

Focusing on the soul's true nature after invoking the five supreme beings — Arihant, Siddha, Acharya, Upadhyaya, Sadhu. The Navkar is not just a prayer — it is a launchpad for soul contemplation.

Meditation 06 ENGLISH

Meditation on Impermanence — Anitya Bhavna

By Varun Jain

Contemplating impermanence — the first of the 12 Bhavnas. Everything in the world is transient. Relationships, bodies, circumstances — all passing. Only the soul is eternal.

Meditation 07 ENGLISH · GUJARATI

Venerating the Virtues of the Five Supreme Beings

By Rakesh Jhaveri

Contemplating and absorbing the qualities of the Panch Parmeshthi — not just reciting their names but becoming what they represent. Available in both English and Gujarati.

Meditation 08 UNIVERSAL 🌐

Navkar Mantra — Seven Chakra Activation

By Music Mantra

Navkar Mantra chant designed to activate and align the seven chakras — where Jain mantra meets universal energy work. A powerful entry point for seekers from any background.

Meditation 09 UNIVERSAL 🌐

Jinoham Stotra — Stotra Chanting

By Jain Media

A deeply meditative chanting of Jinoham Stotra — "I am Jina, I am the victorious soul." Sanskrit stotra, no comprehension needed. Pure vibrational experience. The soul recognizing itself as already victorious.

Meditation 10 HINDI

मैं पूर्ण हूं — I Am Complete

By Arun Vijay MS

Contemplating the soul's inherent completeness and perfection beyond karma. मैं द्रव्य स्वरूप से पूर्ण हूं — I am complete in my essential nature. मैं गुणों से पूर्ण हूं — I am complete in all my qualities.

Meditation 11 HINDI

मैं अनंत हूं — I Am Infinite

By Arun Vijay MS

The soul as a source of infinite perception, infinite knowledge, infinite bliss, and infinite energy — infinite and perfect beyond the clouds of karma accumulated on the soul.

Meditation 12 HINDI

मैं देह से भिन्न हूं — I Am Separate From My Body

By Arun Vijay MS

The soul is not the body. The body is in the soul's possession — not the other way around. This meditation guides you to experience that separation directly and permanently.

"The soul that knows itself needs nothing else to know."

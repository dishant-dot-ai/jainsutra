Jiva
जीव

The living soul—conscious, eternal, and capable of liberation.

Every living being has a Jiva—a soul with consciousness as its defining quality. It is the vital energy, the sentient essence that experiences the world through the prism of existence.

"The soul is the master of its own destiny. It is neither created nor destroyed, but exists in a continuous state of becoming until it finds the silence of its own light."

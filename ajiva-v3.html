Ajiva

"Everything that is not the soul—matter, space, time, motion, and rest."

The Non-Living Realm

Ajiva represents the vast totality of existence that lacks consciousness (Chetana). While the soul (Jiva) is the observer, Ajiva provides the stage, the tools, and the medium through which the soul interacts with reality.

"Matter, space, time, and the principles of motion and rest are the pillars of the Ajiva Tattva."

Adharma

The medium of rest. Like the shade of a tree allows a traveler to stop, Adharma aids in stability.

Kaal: The Measure of Change

Kaal or Time is the substance that facilitates transformation. It is the silent catalyst for the aging of bodies and the shifting of seasons. Unlike the other four, Kaal is often described as an 'Astikaya'—a substance without a spatial body.

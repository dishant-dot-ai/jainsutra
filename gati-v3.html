Gati: The Cycle of Becoming

The four states of existence a soul cycles through until it finds the path of ultimate liberation.

dark_mode State I

Narak — Hellish Beings

The hellish realm where souls endure suffering due to past violence, anger, and excessive accumulation.

Hellish Beings
nature State II

Tiryanch — Animal Kingdom (Micro to Macro)

The animal and plant kingdoms. A state marked by basic survival instincts and varying levels of sensory consciousness.

person State III

Manushya — Human Beings

The rarest and most auspicious state. Only in the human form can a soul practice restraint, tap into deep wisdom, and eventually break the cycle of Gati.

brightness_7 State IV

Dev — Heavenly Beings

Celestial beings living in heavens. While a state of immense pleasure and longevity, it is still temporary and bound by the laws of Karma.

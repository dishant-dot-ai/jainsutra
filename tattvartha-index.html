Masterclass — Sacred Texts

Tattvartha­sutra (तत्त्वार्थसूत्र)

The Compendium of Reality. Ten chapters that map the entire Jain universe, the nature of the soul, and the path to ultimate freedom.

Chapter 01 arrow_outward

Faith and Knowledge

The foundational path to liberation through the Three Jewels: right faith, knowledge, and conduct.
Chapter 02 arrow_outward

The Living World

The biology of the soul: classifying living beings by their senses, bodies, and inner qualities.
Chapter 03 arrow_outward

The Lower and Middle Worlds

A map of the cosmos, detailing the seven hells and the continents where humans and animals dwell.
Chapter 04 arrow_outward

The Celestial Realms

Exploring the four classes of heavenly beings and the structure of the higher worlds.
Chapter 05 arrow_outward

Non-Living Substances

The physics of reality: space, time, matter, and the mediums of motion and rest.
Chapter 06 arrow_outward

The Influx of Karma

How our thoughts, words, and actions act as a magnet for karmic matter.
Chapter 07 arrow_outward

The Five Vows

Practical ethics for a spiritual life: from non-violence to non-attachment.
Chapter 08 arrow_outward

Bondage of Karma

The mechanics of how karma sticks to the soul and decides our future experiences.
Chapter 09 arrow_outward

Stoppage and Shedding

The science of purification: how to block new karma and dissolve the old through discipline.
Chapter 10 arrow_outward

Liberation

The ultimate goal: the soul's upward journey to eternal bliss and perfect knowledge.

Jiva — The Living Soul

जीव

Transliteration: Jīva

"The living soul — conscious, eternal, and capable of liberation."

Internal Vision

Visualization of the Atman radiating pure awareness within the void of matter.

The Anatomy of
Consciousness

01

Pure Infinitude

Every living being has a Jiva — a soul with consciousness as its defining quality. The soul is not the body, not the mind, not the karma attached to it.

02

Infinite Awareness

It is pure, infinite awareness underneath it all. While the body decays and the mind wavers, the Jiva remains an unchanging observer of the temporal dance.

"The eye through which I see the universe is the same eye through which the universe sees me."

— Ancient Siddhanta Scroll

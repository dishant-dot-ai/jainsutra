The Process

"All three must be understood together."

The Sacred Mechanics

Asrav is the inflow of karma through activity. Samvar is stopping that inflow through vows, carefulness, and equanimity. Nirjara is actively burning away existing karma through austerity and meditation.

01

Asrav (Inflow)

The aperture through which the world enters the soul. Every thought, word, and action creates a ripple, pulling karmic matter into the self.

02

Samvar (Stoppage)

The spiritual seal. Through mindfulness and the five Great Vows, the seeker closes the gates, preventing further accumulation.

03

Nirjara (Shedding)

The alchemical fire. Through Tapas (austerity), the soul burns off historical layers of karma, becoming lighter and more luminous.

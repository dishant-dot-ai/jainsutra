COSMIC DIAGRAM // LOK AKASH
The Central Anchor
Pure awareness amidst the rotating cycles of Prakriti

Beyond the
Biological
Frame.

NON-BODY

The soul is not the carbon structure it inhabits, nor the sensory organs it utilizes.

NON-MIND

Thoughts are atmospheric events. The Jiva is the sky in which they appear.

PURE AWARENESS

Its defining quality is Cetana — the light of consciousness that illuminates data.

"The soul is not the body, not the mind, not the karma attached to it."

In the structural plan of the cosmos, every living being possesses a Jiva. It is the permanent observer in a temporary universe. While the physical form undergoes entropy and the mental form undergoes fluctuation, the Jiva remains constant.

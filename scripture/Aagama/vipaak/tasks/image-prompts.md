Place generated images in: `/Aagama/vipaak/assets/`

Also needed for index pages:
- `/Aagama/assets/vipaak-hero.jpg` — for aagama-index.html Vipaak card
- `/Aagama/assets/uttaradhyana-hero.jpg` — for aagama-index.html Uttaradhyayana card

---

## Style Guide (for all Vipaak images)

**Visual style:** Dark, muted, painterly. Cinematic lighting. Ancient India aesthetic.
**Color palette:** Deep ochres, burnt golds, earth tones, shadow.
**No text in images.** Landscape aspect ratio preferred (4:3 or 16:9).
**Mood for Duhkhavipak:** somber, dramatic, heavy, ancient suffering
**Mood for Sukhavipak:** warm, luminous, elevated, serene joy

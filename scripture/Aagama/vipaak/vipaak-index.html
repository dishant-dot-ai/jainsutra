Aagama Sutra · Eleventh Anga

Vipaak (विपाक)

The Scripture of Karmic Ripening. Twenty stories — ten of suffering from past evil, ten of joy from past virtue — demonstrating exactly how karma ripens across lifetimes.

Author Lord Mahavira
Sections 2 Canons
Chapters 20 Total
Status All Available

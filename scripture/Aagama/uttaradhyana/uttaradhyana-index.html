Aagama Sutra · Eleventh Anga

Uttaradhyayana (उत्तराध्ययन)

The Final Teachings of Lord Mahavira from his last 48 hours of continuous preaching before attaining Nirvana. Thirty-six chapters mapping the path of renunciation, the mechanics of karma, and the ultimate nature of the soul.

Teachings By

Lord Mahavira

Compiled By

Arya Sudharmashvami

#5 Chief Disciple of the Lord

Translated By

Dishant Shah

36 Total

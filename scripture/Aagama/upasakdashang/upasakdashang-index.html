Aagama Sutra · Seventh Upānga

Upasakdashang उपासकदशांग

The Scripture of Ten Exemplary Lay Followers. Ten chapters tracing the lives, vows, divine tests, and spiritual triumphs of ten householders who practiced Lord Mahāvīra's teachings while living in the world. The definitive guide to the Jain lay path.

Teachings By

Lord Mahāvīra

Compiled By

Arya Sudharmashvami

Translated By

Dishant Shah

Chapters

10 Total

Chapter 01 arrow_outward

Lay Follower Ānanda

A wealthy merchant of Champā transforms through twelve vows and eleven stages of practice.
Chapter 02 arrow_outward

Lay Follower Kāmadeva

Divine testing by Indra in terrifying forms — demon, elephant, serpent — all resisted through fearlessness.
Chapter 03 arrow_outward

Lay Follower Chulanīpitā

A celestial being threatens his mother — testing whether love or spiritual resolve is stronger.
Chapter 04 arrow_outward

Lay Follower Surādeva

The only devotee who wavers — and is restored by his wife Dhanna's wisdom. Proof that recovery is possible.
Chapter 05 arrow_outward

Lay Follower Chullanishataka

Divine testing, physical suffering, and the unshakeable power of a supportive spouse in Alabhika.
Chapter 06 arrow_outward

Lay Follower Kundkolik

The triumph of human effort over fatalism and the unshakeable resolve of choice in Kampillyapur.
Chapter 07 arrow_outward

Lay Follower Sakdalputra

The potter of Polaspur who discovered spiritual agency, renouncing fatalism for the Jain path of effort.
Chapter 08 arrow_outward

Lay Follower Mahashatak

Detachment amidst domestic indulgence and the extraordinary support of ancient masters in Rajgrihi.
Chapter 09 arrow_outward

Lay Follower Nandinipita

The power of quiet persistence — twenty years of steady, obstacle-free devotion in Shravasti.
Chapter 10 arrow_outward

Lay Follower Salihipita

The completion of the ten paths and the formal conclusion of the sacred Upasakdashang scripture.

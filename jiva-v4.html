The Living Essence

Jiva (जीव)

"The Conscious Soul"

The living soul — conscious, eternal, and capable of liberation. It is pure, infinite awareness underneath it all.

The Essence of Jiva

01

Upayoga: Pure Consciousness

Upayoga is the defining characteristic of Jiva. It is the manifestation of consciousness through perception (Darshana) and knowledge (Jnana).

02

Amurta: The Formless

While residing in a physical body, the soul itself is non-material (Amurta). It possesses no taste, color, smell, or touch.

03

Kartr: The Agent

The Jiva is the doer of its own actions and the reaper of its own karmic fruits, traversing the cycles of existence until liberation.

"Just as a lamp illuminates a room, the soul illuminates the entire body with consciousness."
— Tattvartha Sutra

Eternal Continuity

The soul was never created, and it can never be destroyed. It transitions through forms like a traveler through stations.

flare

Infinite Potency

Every Jiva possesses the inherent capacity for infinite knowledge, perception, and bliss.

The Three States

  • Bahiratman (External Soul)
  • Antaratman (Internal Soul)
  • Paramatman (Supreme Soul)
Awareness

Anekantavada

Understanding the soul through multifaceted reality. The soul is both permanent in its essence and changing in its modes (Paryayas).

The Spiritual Path

गुणस्थानक

The 14 stages of spiritual evolution—a meditative journey from the depths of total ignorance to the infinite peak of complete liberation.

"As the soul sheds its karmic weight, it rises like a flame through the fourteen dimensions of awareness."

The Ascent of the Soul

Mapping the internal transformation from Mithyatva to Moksha.

01-03: The Awakening

Emerging from the darkness of false perception.

01
Mithyatva

Stage of false belief. The soul is in total ignorance, identified with the body and external world.

02
Saswadana

The falling from right belief. A brief, lingering taste of truth before slipping back into illusion.

03
Mishra

Mixed belief. A state of oscillation between truth and falsehood, where the soul seeks clarity.

04
Avirata Samyaktva

Right perception without vows. Knowing the truth but lacking the discipline to act upon it.

05
Desavirata

Partial vows. The beginning of self-restraint and ethical living in daily world affairs.

06
Pramatta Sanyata

Full vows with negligence. Commitment to the path, but occasionally hindered by minor laxity.

04-06: The Discipline

Translating internal vision into external action through vows.

07-10: The Sublimation

The refining of the subtle internal energies and passions.

07
Apramatta Sanyata

Vows without negligence. Total mindfulness and continuous awareness in every breath.

08
Apurva Karana

New spiritual experiences. The soul encounters internal states never before witnessed.

09
Anivritti Karana

Advanced thought activity. The elimination of gross passions through intense meditation.

10
Sukshma Samparaya

Subtle passion. Only the faintest trace of greed remains, like the scent of a flower.

11
Upashanta Moha

Suppressed delusion. Passions are not eliminated but temporary calmed, like sediment in water.

12
Kshina Moha

Eliminated delusion. Passions are destroyed forever. The soul becomes pure and unshakeable.

13
Sayogi Kevali

Omniscience with activity. The Tirthankara state—infinite knowledge while still in a physical body.

14
Ayogi Kevali

Final Omniscience. The cessation of all activity just before the final leap into Moksha.

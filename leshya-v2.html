The Alchemy of Intent

"Your thoughts, intentions, and actions right now are coloring your soul."

The Luminosity of Spirit

In Jain metaphysics, Leshya is the term used for the mental state or the coloring of the soul. It is the bridge between our active passions (Kashaya) and the influx of new Karma.

Spiritual progress is literally brightening. As we shed greed, anger, and delusion, the vibrational frequency of the soul shifts, moving through six distinct stages of purification.

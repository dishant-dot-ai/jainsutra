GATI

The Four States of Existence

The four currents of migration. A study on the perpetual wandering of the soul through the dimensions of existence.

01.

Narak Hellish Beings

The realms of intense suffering, not as a punishment from a deity, but as the mathematical gravity of heavy karma. Here, the soul experiences the fruition of violence and profound attachment.

"A landscape of internal shadows manifesting as external heat and cold."

02.

Tiryanch Sub-humans

From the single-sensed life in a drop of water to the complex instincts of the wild. The state of Tiryanch represents the struggle for survival, governed by the primary instincts of food, fear, and procreation.

It is the vast majority of life on the planet—a cycle of being that is often overlooked yet fundamental to the cosmic balance of Ahimsa.

Mortal

03.

Manushya

The rarest opportunity. The only state from which one can achieve liberation (Moksha). In the human form, we possess the unique combination of discernment (Viveka) and the capacity for penance.

The bridge between the beast and the divine.

04.

Dev Celestial Beings

Often mistaken for the final destination, the celestial realm is but a high-end plateau. Beings here live in immense pleasure and longevity, yet they too are bound by the cycle of birth and death.

4 Ghati (Destructive)

Jnanavarniya

Knowledge-obscuring

Veils the natural omniscience of the soul, like a cloth covering a mirror.

Darshanavarniya

Perception-obscuring

Prevents the soul from perceiving the true nature of reality and divine vision.

Mohniya

Delusory

The most dangerous karma; it confuses the soul, causing attachment and aversion.

Antaraya

Power-obscuring

Obstructs the innate power and energy of the soul to perform good deeds.

4 Aghati (Non-destructive)

Vedniya

Feeling-producing

Determines the physical and worldly pleasure or pain experienced by the being.

Nama

Body-determining

Determines the physical body, its shape, color, and sensory capabilities.

Gotra

Status-determining

Determines the family, social environment, and spiritual lineage of birth.

Ayu

Life-span-determining

Determines the specific duration of a soul's life in its current physical form.

The Mechanics of Binding

Karma in Jainism is literal — fine particles of matter that bind to the soul. It is not an abstract tally of deeds, but a physical weight that determines the soul's trajectory through the universe.

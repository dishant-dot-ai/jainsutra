THE THIRD JEWEL

चारित्र

Charitra

Definition

The conduct that aligns the soul with its inherent purity.

Right Conduct—not rules, but the natural behavior of a soul moving toward freedom.

Charitra is the culmination of the Ratnatraya (Three Jewels). While Right Vision (Samyak Darshana) provides the path and Right Knowledge (Samyak Jnana) lights the way, Right Conduct is the actual walking of that path.

It is not a collection of restrictive laws, but a liberating alignment where action and essence become one. As the soul sheds its karmic weight, these behaviors arise not by effort, but by nature.

"Asceticism is not the rejection of life, but the perfection of conduct."

ANCIENT INSIGHT

spa

Maintaining balance amidst the duality of pleasure and pain, success and failure, on the journey to the self.

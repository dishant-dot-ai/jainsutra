नव तत्त्व

The Nine Realities

A profound framework explaining the soul's journey from worldly bondage to ultimate spiritual liberation.

"The universe is composed of Jiva and Ajiva. Understanding their interaction is the first step toward the cessation of suffering."

Tattvartha Sutra
01. Jiva

The Living Being

The conscious soul characterized by knowledge, perception, and infinite energy. It is the eternal witness of the cosmic drama.

Consciousness Eternal
02. Ajiva

Non-Living Matter

The non-conscious substances including space, time, and matter that provide the environment for the soul's experience.

category
03. Punya

Virtue

star_shine

Meritorious actions leading to worldly comfort and favorable rebirth, acting as a gentle wind in the soul's journey.

04. Pap

Demerit

cloud

Harmful actions that bind the soul to suffering and lower realms of existence, obscuring the soul's inherent light.

05. Asrav

Inflow

The process by which karmic matter enters the soul through the channels of activity, desire, and attachment.

06. Bandh

Bondage

The actual attachment of karmic particles to the soul, much like dust sticking to an oily surface.

07. Samvar

Stoppage

The intentional blocking of new karma through mindfulness, vows, and disciplined spiritual practice.

08. Nirjara

Shedding

The removal of accumulated karma through austerity and deep meditation, purifying the soul's essence.

09. Moksha

The State of Liberation

The ultimate destination. Complete freedom from the cycle of birth and death, where the soul exists in eternal bliss and infinite knowledge.

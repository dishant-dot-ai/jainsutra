Metaphysics of Existence

Six Dravyas (षड् द्रव्य)

"The Substances of Reality"

The six pillars that make up all of existence — nothing more, nothing less.

The Eternal Components

Fundamental Principles

01 psychology

Jiva

The Living Soul. Characterized by consciousness (Chetana). The only substance that perceives and experiences.

02 grain

Pudgal

Matter and Energy. Anything that can be seen, heard, felt, smelled or tasted. Subject to integration and disintegration.

03 directions_run

Dharma

The Medium of Motion. It does not move things itself, but provides the necessary medium for souls and matter to move.

04 accessibility_new

Adharma

The Medium of Rest. Like the shade of a tree for a traveler, it is the passive catalyst that allows objects to remain still.

05 blur_on

Akash

Space. That which provides room or accommodation to all other substances. It is infinite and all-pervasive.

06 schedule

Kaal

Time. The medium of change or transformation. While not having 'Pradesha' (extension), it governs all becoming.

format_quote

"Nothing is newly created, nothing is totally destroyed. Reality is the constant dance of these six eternal substances changing their modes."

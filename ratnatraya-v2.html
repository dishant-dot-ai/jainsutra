The Three Jewels

Ratnatraya

रत्नत्रय

Three Jewels — Right Faith, Right Knowledge, Right Conduct. Together, they are the complete path. No single jewel alone leads to liberation.

"

All three must arise together — like three legs of a stool. If one is missing, the structure of spiritual liberation collapses.

The Ratnatraya represents the essential synthesis required for the soul's journey toward Samyak (Rightness). It is not enough to simply believe, nor is it enough to simply know. Action without faith is empty, and faith without knowledge is blind. The Digital Alchemist views these as the fundamental code of existence. Samyak Darshan provides the clarity of vision, Samyak Jnan provides the architect's blueprint, and Samyak Charitra provides the steady hand that builds the cathedral of the self.

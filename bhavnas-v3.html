भावना

12 contemplations that rewire how the soul sees itself and the world.

Bhavna is the conscious cultivation of thought. It is the practice of repeatedly reflecting on universal truths to dissolve the ego and realize the eternal nature of the soul.

01. Impermanence

Anitya

Everything in the material world is transient. Like clouds in the sky, youth, wealth, and life itself are constantly shifting.

02. Helplessness

Asharan

In the face of death and karmic consequences, no external power can provide ultimate refuge except the path of truth.

03. Cycle of Birth

Samsara

The soul wanders through infinite lifetimes, driven by desire and attachment, finding no permanent home in the worldly cycle.

self_improvement
04. Solitude

Ekatva

The soul is born alone and departs alone. Happiness and misery are experienced individually. Realizing this solitude leads to true independence.

05. Separateness

Anyatva

The soul is distinct from the body and all material relations. The "I" is the observer, not the shell.

06. Impurity

Asuchi

Contemplating the physical body's nature to reduce vanity.

07. Influx

Asrav

Observing how thoughts and actions attract new karmic matter.

08. Stoppage

Samvar

The art of closing the doors to new karma through mindfulness.

09. Shedding

Nirjara

Purifying the soul by burning away past karmic accumulations.

10. Universe

Loka

Understanding the structure and vastness of the cosmos, recognizing our tiny yet eternal place within it.

11. Rare Enlightenment

Bodhi-durlabha

Recognizing that the opportunity for spiritual growth and right perception is exceedingly rare and precious.

12. The Path

Dharma

Contemplating the liberating nature of the Tirthankara's teachings as the only true boat to cross the ocean of life.

"When the soul meditates on these twelve, the world loses its grip, and the light of true knowledge begins to dawn."

Ancient Wisdom

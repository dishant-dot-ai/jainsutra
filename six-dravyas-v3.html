The Fundamental Reality

षड् द्रव्य

The six substances that make up all of existence—nothing more, nothing less.

जीव brightness_7

Jiva

The living being or soul. Characterized by consciousness, perception, and knowledge. It is the only substance that experiences joy, pain, and liberation.

Sentient Existence
पुद्गल grain

Pudgal

Matter and Energy. Anything that has color, taste, smell, and touch. It can be perceived by the senses.

धर्म

Dharma

The medium of motion. Just as water helps fish to swim, Dharma allows substances to move.

अधर्म

Adharma

The medium of rest. Like the shade of a tree helps a traveler stay still, Adharma assists in stillness.

filter_drama आकाश

Akash

Space. That which provides room for all other substances to exist and interact.

schedule काल

Kaal: The Measure of Change

Time is the catalyst of transformation. It doesn't move itself, but it is the auxiliary cause of change (Parinama) in all other substances.

history

"Nothing is newly created, and nothing is ever destroyed. Existence is simply the eternal dance and transformation of these six fundamental dravyas."

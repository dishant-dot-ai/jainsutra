सम्यक्त्व

Samyaktva: The Right Faith

Right faith—the single doorway to liberation. Samyaktva is the soul's correct self-orientation—recognizing its own nature as separate from karma, body, and external identity.

auto_awesome

Tattva-Artha

Belief in the fundamental principles of reality exactly as they are, without delusion or doubt.

"The vision that sees the self as the self and the non-self as non-self."
— Tattvartha Sutra
filter_vintage

The Eight Qualities

Samyaktva is defined by Nis-shankita (absence of doubt), Nih-kankshita (absence of worldly desire), and Nir-vichikitsa (absence of repulsion).

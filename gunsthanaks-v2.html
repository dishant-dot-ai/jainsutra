The Threshold of Awareness

The transition from the 3rd to the 4th stage marks the single most important moment in a soul's journey. It is where 'Samyaktva' or Right Vision is attained. Before this point, progress is accidental; after this point, liberation is inevitable.

Foundation

Stage 1-3

Bound by delusion and worldly attachment. The soul perceives the unreal as real.

Ascension

Stage 4-12

Disciplined purification. The slow shedding of karmic weight through awareness.

The Eternal Climb

"As the alchemist purifies lead into gold, the soul purifies its perception into omniscience."

Structure of Evolution

Siddhanta Analysis

I

Delusion

The state of Mithyatva. Spiritual darkness where the self is identified entirely with the physical body and temporary desires.

II

Vigilance

The intermediate stages of partial vows and self-restraint. The soul begins to experience moments of profound clarity.

III

Purity

Kevala Jnana. The final stages where the soul exists in its natural state of infinite knowledge and bliss, awaiting the final release.

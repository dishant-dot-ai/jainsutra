The Soul's Journey

आस्रव · संवर · निर्जरा

Asrav · Samvar · Nirjara

"The three movements of the soul: The flow, the stillness, and the purification."

Tattva Vidya

waves 01

Asrav

Karma flowing in.

The influx of karmic matter into the soul, caused by mind, speech, and body activities. The beginning of bondage.

pan_tool 02

Samvar

Karma stopped.

The blockage of fresh karmic particles through mindfulness, restraint, and mental discipline. The path to peace.

auto_awesome

Nirjara

Karma shed.

"As the wind blows away the dust from a mirror, penance and meditation shed the accumulated karmas from the soul."

The process of exhausting or falling off of karmas already present in the soul. This is achieved through internal and external tapas (austerities).
